# Demo Setup — Variation Capture

## Prerequisites
- Expo Go installed on your iPhone
- iPhone and Mac on the same WiFi network

## One-time setup
1. Add your Anthropic API key to `app.json` → `extra.anthropicApiKey`
2. Run: `cd variation && npx expo start`
3. Scan the QR code with your iPhone camera

## Demo flow (60 seconds)
1. Open app → 3 projects pre-loaded
2. Tap "Westgate Tunnel — Section 4B"
3. Tap "New Variation" (+ button)
4. Step 1: Tap "Take Photo" → snap a photo of anything
5. Step 1: Tap the mic → say "The superintendent directed us to upgrade the rock class between chainages 4200 and 4350 due to unexpected ground conditions. Estimated cost is $45,000."
6. Tap "Next: Tag"
7. Step 2: Title → "Rock class upgrade — unexpected ground conditions"
8. Step 2: Source → "Verbal Direction"
9. Step 2: Estimated Value → 45000
10. Step 2: Description → "Superintendent directed rock class upgrade due to latent conditions"
11. Tap "Save Variation"
12. Tap into the new variation
13. Tap "Generate" next to AI Description → Claude generates a formal variation notice
14. Tap "Export as PDF" → professional variation notice ready to send

## What impresses people
- GPS and timestamp auto-captured
- Evidence chain hash (tamper-proof)
- AI turns rough site notes into a formal contract document in seconds
- PDF ready to email on the spot
